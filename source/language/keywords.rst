########
Keywords
########

The following keywords are reserved and cannot be used as identifiers.

* ``while``
* ``if``
* ``else``
* ``as``
* ``import``
* ``from``
* ``do``
* ``for``
* ``foreach``
* ``switch``

* ``class``
* ``extends``
* ``implements``
* ``inherits``
* ``abstract``
* ``final``
* ``interface``
* ``const``
* ``static``

* ``public``
* ``private``
* ``protected``
* ``method``
* ``property``

* ``catch``
* ``finally``
* ``throw``
* ``return``
* ``break``
* ``breakelse``
* ``continue``
* ``try``
* ``default``
* ``goto``
* ``case``

* ``self``
* ``parent``

* ``yield``
